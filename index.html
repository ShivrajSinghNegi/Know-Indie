What’s Music Theory, and Why Bother?

Music theory is like the rules of the game for making music. It’s not boring, I promise! Think of it as a toolkit to make your songs sound awesome. You don’t need to be a Mozart to get it just a little knowledge can make a big difference.

Why It’s Important
  1. Makes Writing Songs Easier: You’ll know what sounds good together instead of guessing.
  2. Helps You Jam with Others: No more awkward “what chord is that?” moments.
  3. Saves Time: No more random trial and error—just solid progress.
  4. Stops Writer’s Block: Music theory gives you ideas when you’re stuck.
